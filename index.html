Writing made easy.

Right is a writing app that streamlines writing for mobile, making content producing on mobile phones hassle-free and efficient.

Write Right

It has 3 modes you can switch interchangeably to and from, thus streamlining your workflow.

Writing

The focus is on your words. Just write.

A fullscreen immersion with no distractions, without all the bells and whistles existing in other complex word processors.

Refining

Make your work even better.

Through language and word analytics allowing you to do extra research, your writing can soar to greater heights.

Formatting

Highlight, underscore, emphasize.

Swiping and drawing symbols allows you to easily select and format text through customisable gesture-based interactions.

How does it work?

Writing

All you have to do is type.

Refining

Choose your parameters and tap to change any word.

Formatting

Swipe to select, then do a gesture to format the text.

About us.

We are a trio of students who love writing. We found a visceral flaw in modern writing: laptops and traditional writing do not offer sufficient portability and accessibiliy in this fast-paced digital age. Thus, we turned to smartphones. Yet, smartphone word processing apps simply do not work. They are cumbersome and difficult to use and do not fully use the capabilities of smartphones. Often, typing and formatting text requires one to manually select the text, open the formatting menu, and pick the ‘Bold’ option. Reviewing and improving work also requires multiple apps, potentially breaking the flow of writing.

So, we made Right.
